Dodgeball

An evil witch has cursed you and your friends into anthropomorphic animals. If you don’t reverse the curse, you’ll stay animals forever! In order to beat the witch and break this curse, you must defeat her and her ogre minions in her favorite game: dodgeball. It will be hard to play with paws and hooves instead of hands, but you are determined to win back your humanity -- and destroy this evil witch in the process.

Dodgeball

A spin on the classic game!

You are playing dodgeball. Your teammates and opponents are AI. You can only move around your side of the dodgeball court. If you cross the middle line dividing the court, you will be eliminated. Stay on your side. Your goal is to eliminate all your opponent players. You must pick up dodgeballs and throw them at your opponents. Opponents can only be eliminated if you or your teammates get them out with a ball. Some dodgeballs eliminate players when hit; other balls not only eliminate players, but also hinder the other players around them. Airborne balls are considered “live” and dangerous. Balls mid-bounce are considered live. Balls that are rolling on the ground or in a person’s hand are inert and will pose no danger. You will be eliminated if you are hit by your opponents’ balls (depending on their damage type). Friendly-fire is on. You are able to hit and eliminate your teammates, and vice versa. If you are eliminated by any means, it’s game over.

There are three types of balls. Normal, Ice, and Fire. Ice will freeze players in a large radius. Fire will blow up and kill players within a smaller radius.

There are "intelligent" opponents and teammates that move on their own and will throw at you your other opponents. If you die, you will take over one of your teammates bodies until the game is over.
